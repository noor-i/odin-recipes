Chocolate Granola

Recipe

This is an easy and quick recipe that requires little to no effort. It is gluten-free and dairy-free but can be customized to fit your taste. The main ingredients in this recipe are gluten-free rolled oats, coco powder, coconut oil, and maple sugar and coconut sugar. To begin, take a large mixing bowl and combine all your dry ingredients. After that is done, proceed with your melted coconut oil or oil of choice and maple syrup. After mixing well, your mixture should smell almost like brownie batter. You then will take a large flat baking tray and line it with parchment paper. Spread the mixture evenly and flat across the baking tray and set the oven at 320 degrees farenheit. Bake the oats for 10 minutes and then after stiring half way, set for another 10 minutes. After it's done, make sure to let the granola cool completely and store and enjoy within two weeks. Happy granola!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups gluten-free rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened coco powder
  • 1/2 coconut sugar
  • 1/2 maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil

Steps

  1. Set your oven to 320°F (160°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the gluten-free rolled oats, cocoa powder, salt, and coconut sugar.
  3. Melt the coconut oil (or oil of your choice) and add it to the dry ingredients. Add in the maple syrup and vanilla extract.
  4. Mix everything together until well combined.
  5. Take a large, flat baking tray and line it with parchment paper. Evenly spread the granola mixture across the tray.
  6. Place the tray in the oven and bake for 10 minutes. After 10 minutes, stir the granola to ensure even cooking. Bake for another 10 minutes.
  7. Once baked, allow the granola to cool completely. Store it in an airtight container and enjoy within two weeks.
